WHO I AM

About

I'm a data analyst based in Toronto — the kind who is not satisfied until the numbers actually mean something to the person reading them. I work across the full data stack: cleaning raw inputs, building ETL pipelines, modeling in SQL, and packaging it all into dashboards that make the answer obvious at a glance.

What drives me is the moment a 3-day manual process becomes a 4-hour automated one, or when a 6-page dashboard surfaces a $340K churn risk that nobody had spotted. I hold my work to a simple standard: does it make someone's job measurably easier? If the answer is yes, the engineering was worth it.

I hold a postgraduate diploma in Big Data Analytics and another in Project Management from Georgian College, and a BSc in Mathematics from Gujarat University. Outside of data, you'll find me at industry events — Snowflake World Tour, Google Looker User Group, Women in AI hackathons — staying curious about where the field is going next.
